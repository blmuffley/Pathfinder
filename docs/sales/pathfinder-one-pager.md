## The Problem

- **Undocumented integrations** -- 60-80% of application-to-application connections are unknown to the CMDB, creating blind spots for change management, incident response, and compliance.
- **Stale CMDB data** -- Manual documentation decays within months. Service maps built on spreadsheets are wrong before they're published.
- **Unsustainable manual effort** -- CMDB teams spend thousands of hours per year chasing device inventories, validating relationships, and cleaning up duplicate records.

---

## The Solution

- **eBPF kernel-level discovery** -- Lightweight agents observe actual network traffic at the OS kernel. No application changes. No packet captures. Less than 1% CPU overhead.
- **AI-powered intelligence** -- Claude-driven health scoring, integration summarization, rationalization, and anomaly detection across every discovered connection.
- **Autonomous CMDB operations** -- Eight AI agents continuously deduplicate, validate, and remediate CMDB data. Reduces manual maintenance by 80%+.
- **Clinical extension** -- Purpose-built for healthcare: tiered device discovery (IoT, clinical, life-critical), UKG workforce correlation, compliance automation, and clinical incident response.

## How It Works

**Deploy agents** --> **Discover connections** --> **Classify & score** --> **Enrich with AI** --> **Sync to ServiceNow CMDB**

Agents deploy in minutes via RPM, DaemonSet, or MSI. Discovery begins immediately. The gateway classifies flows, resolves them to CIs, and syncs to ServiceNow -- all within your existing CMDB structure using native `cmdb_ci` extensions.
